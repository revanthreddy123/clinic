E-Resources: On-Campus / Off-Campus (Quick Guide)

The Central Library had its humble early stages as the college of Koneru Lakshmaiah College of Engineering (KLEF) established in 1981-82. The new building is constructed as a greenhouse with natural sunlight inside the library.

The Central Library holds a large number of documents and is enriched with more than 1,90,000 books and 32,000 non-book materials including well-reputed databases. It holds materials relevant to the Engineering, Science, and Humanities courses and includes Agricultural Engineering, Pharmacy, Architecture, Law, and specialized books and journals.

A new building has been facilitated on par with international standards with modern facilities. The Library is centralized Air Conditioned for providing library users comfortable experience of reading and learning. The Central Library is proud to share that it is fully automated using KOHA open source software and RFID technology. Patrons can access the subscribed E-resources by using KLEF E-Library Mobile APP.

More than 1000 users can accommodate and enjoy learning and reading facilities in the study rooms, discussion rooms, and open reading hall
