KLEEE Application 2022

Key points

1. The online application form is for admission into different programmes offered in KL University at Hyderabad, Vijayawada.

2. Application Fee is Rs.1000/-

3. Please submit a working E-mail ID for all future correspondence

4. NRI / PIO / OCI candidates are also eligible to apply.

5. You are free to edit your application form except Email ID and mobile number

6. Your choice of program preference is for statistical purpose only. Final decision of your choice of program would be finalized at the time of counseling

7. Entrance Exam, Counseling and course commencement dates will be scheduled as per the Government norms

Schedule
