Faculty

Our faculty is our strength. They are highly educated (doctorate degree holders) and experienced in academia and industry as well. Each faculty member stresses on imparting great deal of classroom and practical knowledge to our students to make them future-ready. Not only teaching, they are also involved in high-end research and contribute to science and technology.

Dr M. Sudhamani, Ph.D

Dr.M. Sudhamani is doctorate from the National Dairy Research Institute. Earlier he has done graduation and post-graduation from Acharya NG Ranga Agricultural University. He was a DAAD fellow and pursued research at the BFEL, Kiel, Germany. Dr. Sudhamani’s interests include food safety and food biotechnology. He has expertise in lactic acid bacteria and their genetic manipulation. Also, he is involved in standardizing fortification technology for trace metals and raising awareness in rural masses regarding Hidden hunger.



Dr A. Kadam, Ph.D

Dr. Kadam obtained his M.S. in packaging from Michigan State University, USA and Ph.D. in Food Packaging from University of Dijon, France. He has expertise in the area of food packaging and holds 9+ years of experience including that in pharmaceutical packaging.Dr. Kadam has 13 articles to his credit published in international peer-reviewed journals.



Dr K. Hemamalini, Ph.D.

Dr. Kola Hemamalini has completed her PhD in Food & Nutritional Sciencesfrom Acharya Nagarjuna University, PG in Food Science and Nutrition from SriVenkateswara University, Tirupathi, and postgraduate Diploma in Nutrition &Dietetics from Sri Padmavathi University. She has 25 years of teaching andadministration experience. She started her career as Lecturer in Food Science& Nutrition for UG programs in GVR & S Degree & PG College for Womenand at present she is working as Assistant Professor and Nutritionist in“KoneruLakshmaiah Education Foundation”, Vijayawada, AP, and India. Sheattended various National and International conferences and presented herresearch papers. She published over 10 research and review articles ininternational journals, out of which 4 are from SCOPUS indexed journals. Sheattended twice to USA to attend international conferences as a speaker. Shecompleted a project sanctioned by Indian Government worth of INR26,13,275/-. She is good in communication and even she orientedadministrative rolls perfectly with her skills and hard work.



Karampudi Padmavathy.

Motivated by the concept of wellness and preventive health, Karampudi Padmavathy, a Holistic Nutrition Practitioner, has done a master’s degree in Home science from Sri Padmavathi Mahila University, Tirupati and B.Ed. from Acharya Nagarjuna University, Guntur. She also has done one year Certification course on Holistic Nutrition. She started her career as a B – grade teaching faculty in Nagarjuna University. She has over 22 years of experience in teaching and administration. She has done certification courses in Mushroom Culture and Terrace Gardening to empower the rural women economically and nutritionally to fit in the society. She reversed over 25 PCOD and Thyroid cases as a Slimming Manage (Dietician) at VLCC. She handled the projects related to Nutrition and Psychological health as a Project co-ordinator. She attended various National and International workshops and conferences. She Participated in Smart village Revolution Activities at KLEF with focus on the Health Domain in rural areas. She has three international journal publications. She wrote a Book on “Millet Recipes” in regional and in English languages. At present she is extending her services for Agricultural Department and Food Technology Department as an Assistant Professor. She is doing her Ph.D



Ms. Tejaswi Boyapati

Ms. Tejaswi Boyapati graduated with an M.Tech (Food Processing Technology) and a B.Tech (Food Technology). She specializes in non-thermal processing technologies, food safety, and analytical quality control. She worked as an intern at CSIR - Central Food Technological Research Institute, Mysuru, and ITC - Agri business division. She has also interned for Sangam Milk Producer Company Limited and Hindusthan Coca-Cola Beverages Pvt. Ltd. She has 7 articles and book chapters published in national and international peer-reviewed journals.
