About Head of the Department

Dr.K.Shrinivasulu P.hD

HOD

Dr. K. Shrinivasulu has been working in this Institute since 2006. He joined as Assistant Professor in the department of biotechnology. During his tenure as HOD between 2014 and 2018, he expanded the department in all ways from infrastructure, research projects, placements, and publications. For instance, a DST-FIST grant worth of 80 lakhs was sanctioned. He is an able administrator with multiple skill sets, creative efficiency, quicker grasping, ability of analytical decision making, highly adaptable and flexible to changing circumstances, good Inter-personal skills, positive attitude, and devotion with 22 years of teaching experience.


He is instrumental for starting the department of pharmacy in the university. He has also worked for admissions from 2009 onwards. He conducted admissions, branding, and promotional activities like digital marketing for the university. Dr. Shrinivasulu has also been spreading awareness about various academic programs through parents/students counseling sessions which are covered by television channels and newspapers from time to time. He is an active member of various internal committees namely NBA, NAAC, and UGC. He obtained his bachelor degree from ANU, Guntur in the year 1997 and master’s in MICROBIOLOGY from SRTMU, Nanded in March 2000. He obtained Ph.D. from ANU, GUNTUR DURING 2013. He Attended and conducted many International and National Conferences and published 38 papers in International Journals for his credentials. He is acting as a BOS member for various autonomous colleges. Cancer biology, microbial secondary metabolites, edible food coatings are his research areas. He acted as the CO-PI for women technology park project funded by DST worth of 90 lakhs.

Prof. Shrinivasulu has provided his able guidance to 2 Ph.D. candidates. One among them is Dr. Shanthi, who is currently working as Assistant Professor for Osmania University. The other candidate Dr. Vignesh is working as Assistant Professor for Yogivemana University. Moreover, five new Ph.D. students have joined under Prof. Shrinivasulu
