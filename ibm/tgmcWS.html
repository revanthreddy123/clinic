TGMC workshops conducted by students/colleges

The Student ambassadors of KLUniversity Mr. L Karthick and Ch. Sarat have conducted various technical sessions on TGMC 2011 contest in the month of July and August for 3rd and 4th year students of CSE, IST, ECM, MCA, ECE and EEE. The topics included are

  1. TGMC Registration
  2. Selection of TGMC Project scenario
  3. Completion of SRS for the projects selected
  4. Submission of Final executable file

The ambassadors assured and guaranteed the students to provide the support needed for successful completion of TGMC project.

Prof K Raja Sekhar, SPOC of IBM Center of Excellence has chaired all the workshop sessions.
