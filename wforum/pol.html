Policies

KL University Women's Forum (KLUWF) was constituted in the month of Sepetember 2010.

Ideological aspects of KLUWF:

KLUWF shall promote policies following UGC norms.

Pro Women:

Supporting women to identify, explore and unleash their potential.

Anti-Caste and Pro-Secularism :

Inculcating religious tolerance.

Anti-Politics:

Staying away from any party politics, yet mobilization of women issues affecting them.

Intenal Complaints Committee:

KLU ICC

KL_Prevention_of_Sexual_Harassment_Policy.

Measures for Ensuring the Safety of Women.

Objectives of KLUWF:

To create a platform for all women in the university.

To address critical issues and needs of female employees and students.

To encourage and develop entrepreneurial skills in women.

To mobilize women in the university towards various educational, cultural, economic and social actions.

Modus operandi of women's forum:

Women Entrepreneur Cell

Women's Day Celebrations

Section-wise events

Formation of Student groups

Arranging drop boxes
