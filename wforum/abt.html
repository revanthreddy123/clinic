About Us

Have you ever heard about Indra Nooyi , Kiran Bedi , Kalpana Chawla , P.T.Usha , Nirupama Rao or are you wondering who these people are ? They are the women who made their mark in history . If you want to be like them , here is an opportunity for you .

VISION ::

Women Forum envisions fostering and developing an environment where women develop the passion of excelling themselves in their interested fields .

MISSION ::

The mission of Women's Forum is to make the women vigilant of developing their confidence and personality . It also inspires women through Guest lectures , Workshops and and many more competetions to sheen the innovative ideas into start ups .
