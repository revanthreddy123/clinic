B.Tech Degree with Honors

A student is eligible for B. Tech Degree with honors subject to the following.

  1. S/he should have a CGPA of 8.5 or higher at the end of semester - 4.
  2. S/he must pursue 5 additional courses, (covering not less than 20 credits) other than the courses required as per program, by separately registering for those courses.
  3. S/he must pursue the additional courses by overloading during a semester or summer term.
  4. S/he is eligible for the degree with honors only if CGPA of 8.5 or higher is maintained in each subsequent semester/term without attempting betterment after registering for Degree with Honors.
  5. In case a student fails to meet the CGPA requirement for Degree with Honors at any point after registration, s/he will be dropped from the list of students eligible for Degree with Honors and they will receive B.Tech Degree only. However such students will receive a separate grade sheet mentioning the additional courses completed by them.

  4. Bachelor of Technology (Honors) in Computer Science & Engineering (CSE)
  5. Bachelor of Technology (Honors) in Computer Science & Information Technology (CS & IT)
  6. Bachelor of Technology (Honors) in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
  7. Bachelor of Technology (Honors) in Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE)
  8. Bachelor of Technology (Honors) in Electronics and Computer Engineering (ECM)
  9. Bachelor of Technology (Honors) in Mechanical Engineering (ME)
