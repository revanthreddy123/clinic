B.Tech Degree with a Minor

A student who fulfills the B. Tech program requirements of a discipline in which s/he was admitted, is awarded a B.Tech degree in that discipline. The KLEF also offers flexibility for a student to successfully complete five (5) additional courses (necessarily comprising of professional core courses category) from another discipline, which collectively accounts to 20 credits. Having done so s/he gets eligibility for the award of a minor degree in that discipline.
