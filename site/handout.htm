Handout

Handout is a document which gives complete plan of the course. It consists of rationale and objectives of the course, Course outcomes and Course outcome indicators and their mapping to program outcomes and program specific outcomes , team of instructors (in case of multi-section courses), instructors for theory and practicals, syllabus recommended by Board of Studies, textbook(s) and reference books, Course delivery plan, session plan for allsessions, evaluation scheme (date and venue of evaluation components, weightage), chamber consultancy hour, course notices and other course related matters. In essence, course handout is an agreement between students and teacher. Handouts will be distributed on the first day of the commencement of the programme .

All handouts at KLEF are prepared using Booms Taxonomy and outcomes based education.
