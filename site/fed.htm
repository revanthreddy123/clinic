Freshman Engineering Department

Freshman Engineering Department (FED) has been established with a view to achieve concentrated focus for the smooth migration of students having different backgrounds and varied abilities at plus 2 level to the professional studies by inculcating practice oriented training. The concept of a Freshman Engineering Department is meant to cater to the needs of all new entrants providing them a well organized platform for their academic and social development. Another unique features is the Communication and Soft Skills department which has been specially designed to cater to the requirements of the individual students in honing their life-skills and communication abilities. These additional skills address the requirements of modern day professionals and technocrats to acquire the best jobs available and also to achieve overall development.
