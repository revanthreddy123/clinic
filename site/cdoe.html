KLU CENTRE FOR DISTANCE & ONLINE EDUCATION(KLUCDOE)

Koneru Lakshmaiah Education Foundation (Deemed to be University) is a CATEGORY-1 University with NAAC A++ Grade which is duly recognized by the UGC under Section 3 of UGC act 1956. KLU Centre for Distance & Online Education (KLUCDOE) was initiated by KLEF and is affiliated to the same.

KLU Centre for Distance & Online Education (KLUCDOE) , Distance & Online Learning wing of KLEF is initiated with an objective of offering a wide range of Academic Programs with a well-structured learner Centric Support System to the students of various ages, needs and locations. It is striving to extend the reach of higher education to different sections of the society located in divergent geographical areas with a mission to offers educational services using operational and academic principles that optimize academic quality, relevant to present needs of Industry, Society and Learners., Cost-effective and flexible.

Students admitted under Distance /Online Learning under KLUCDOE study the same syllabi and are subject to the same regulations as that of the regular students of KLEF. Their learning package comprises of self-learning study material, audio-video packages and face-to-face interaction.

KLUCDOE offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate degree programmes in many diverse disciplines such as Management, Commerce, Arts, Computer Applications etc. under both Distance and Online Mode of Education. KLUCDOE open admissions twice an year and the Programs commences in July and January of every academic year.

Under Distance Mode of Education KLUCDOE offers the following Programs

Under Graduate Programs

    a. Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)
    b. Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)
    c. Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com)
    d. Bachelor of Arts (BA)
    e. Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.)

Post Graduate programs

    a. Master of Arts - MA ( English)
    b. Master of Science - M.Sc.(Mathematics)

Under Online Mode of Education, KLUCDOE is offering the following Programs

    b. Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)
    c. Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com)

Post Graduate programs
