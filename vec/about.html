About Cell

All Human Beings aspire for a continuous of happiness and prosperity fulfilling and successful life. The continuous happiness is naturally acceptable to every individual. This is part and parcel of every human being; no one needs to verify this but every human being enable to refer with right understanding to reach our aspiration. The prevailing notion of human beings due to lack of right understanding, most of the human beings feel that “Happiness and sad are two sides of the same coin” or “Happiness and sadness are two banks of the same river.

The long history of mankind has seen a lot of progress. Science has enabled us to improve understanding of the physical reality around us. We have travelled a long way from the time of warring tribes and the fear of natural elements. While we have made tremendous advancement and innovations, the question still remains- Are we satisfied with the state of affairs today? On looking into this issue, it becomes evident that we are still faced with serious problems at various levels. At human level, we can see that there continue to be issues in individual with respect to lack of clarity on life-goals, emotional and behavioral problem in individuals leads to which leads to contradiction, stress, depress, suicide and health related problems and insecurity, while at the level of human relationship in the family, we see problems in mutual understanding generation gap, increasing mistrust, divorce rate, dowry death, neglect of older people. Further, at the level of human relationship in society we see increasing communal conflict, exploitation of strife, terrorism and violence in various forms. The increasing corruption and other unethical practice in various profession is also causing serious concern. At the level of nature, we have increasing of problems of environmental degradation in various forms as well as resource depletion threatening the very survival of human race. All the above problems are day by day increasing due to lack or right understanding in the self.

When we look around, we find other human beings; we find animal, plants, birds, insects etc and things like water, soil, metals, stone etc. here, we have to identify that each and every entity has co-existence with other entity. Every entity has co-existence in this nature except human being cause of lacking right understanding in the self. Due to this reason human beings have disharmony with entire existence. Human beings are able to fulfill this gap with right understanding. Hence, the right understanding will be possible through “education and samskar” only. Whereas, the present education system focused on how to do part rather than what and why. Here, how do is only focused on skill and technology. Hence, what to do is concern for value education. The value education deals with what is universally valuable to all of us. This is mainly focuses on the importance of values of education, human basic aspiration of continuous happiness and prosperity. And other side, this value education awakens the harmony in four levels of the self, family, society and nature/existence with right understanding. This will makes the change on human thought, behavior, work & realization.
